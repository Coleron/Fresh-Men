Overview

Jeddah Economic City or Kingdom City is a unique urban development positioned on the north east coastline of Jeddah
, Saudi Arabia, led by the Jeddah Economic Company. The designated land is over five million square meters in area
and is aimed to become a smartly planned, multilayered network of business, tourism and recreational areas by 2026


Jeddah Tower
Jeddah tower will become the CenterPoint of Jeddah Economic City and will erect to a record high
1000m. This “wonder of the world” will draw tourism to the city, whilst also functioning as a
vertically integrated business area, residential area, commercial area, hotel and recreational area.





Transportation networks

Jeddah economic city will be an efficient city to commute through, as both a multifaceted
underground metro system and an above ground light rail system will be installed.
